BOILED CARROTS

DESCRIPTION

This is a great recipe for great boiled carrots.

INGREDIENTS (for 5 carrots)

  • 5 medium carrots
  • Water
  • Tablespoon of salt

STEPS

  1. Peel your carrots.
  2. Put your carrots into a pot and fill with water until covered and add the salt.
  3. Bring the water to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook for 10-15 min or until soft.
  4. Serve and eat!
