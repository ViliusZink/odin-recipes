HARD BOILED EGG

DESCRIPTION

This is a great recipe for a great hard boiled egg.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 egg
  • Water

STEPS

  1. Fill a pot with enough water to cover your egg and bring it to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat to medium-low, put in your egg and cook for 11 min.
  3. Peel, serve and eat!
