BOILED POTATOES

DESCRIPTION

This is a great recipe for great boiled potatoes.

INGREDIENTS (for 5 potatoes)

  • 5 medium potatoes
  • Water
  • Tablespoon of salt

STEPS

  1. Peel your potatoes.
  2. Put your potatoes into a pot and fill with water until covered and add the salt.
  3. Bring the water to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook for 10-20 min or until soft.
  4. Serve and eat!
